Payment Terms

💳
1. Accepted Payment Methods

We accept a variety of secure and convenient payment options, including:

  • Credit and debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
  • PayPal
  • Bank transfers (details provided upon request)
  • International payment services (such as Wise and Stripe)

📅
2. Payment Schedule

To ensure smooth service delivery, the following payment schedule applies:

  • Standard Orders: Full payment is required before editing work begins.
  • High-Value Orders (USD 300 and above): A 50% deposit may be accepted, with the remaining balance due upon final delivery.
  • Institutional Clients: We offer invoice-based payment options for universities, research centers, and corporate clients, payable within 15 days of the invoice date.

↩️
3. Refund and Cancellation Policy

  • If a project is canceled before editing work has commenced, a full refund will be issued.
  • If work has already begun, a partial refund will be calculated based on the proportion of work completed.
  • All approved refunds will be processed within 7 business days through the original payment method.

⏰
4. Late Payment and Non-Payment

  • Delayed payments may result in the suspension of services or delay in delivery.
  • Persistent non-payment may result in cancellation of ongoing services and legal action if necessary.

💱
5. Currency and Taxes

  • All pricing is listed in U.S. Dollars (USD) unless otherwise stated.
  • Clients are responsible for any applicable local taxes, bank fees, or transaction charges incurred during payment.
