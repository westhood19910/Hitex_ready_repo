Cookie Policy

At HiTex EdiTex, we are committed to ensuring your privacy and transparency in how we collect and use information. This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies on our website, www.hitexeditex.com ("Site"), and how you can manage your cookie preferences.

By using our Site, you consent to our use of cookies in accordance with this Cookie Policy. If you do not agree with the use of cookies, you can adjust your browser settings or discontinue using the Site.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, improve user experience, and provide information to the website operators.

Cookies can be either:

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that expire once you close your browser.
  • Persistent Cookies: Cookies that remain on your device for a set period of time or until you delete them.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies on our Site:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the functionality of the Site and allow you to navigate and use the features, such as accessing secure areas or completing purchases. These cookies do not collect personal information.
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies collect information about how visitors interact with our Site, such as the pages they visit most often, and if they receive error messages. The data collected by these cookies is aggregated and anonymized. We use this information to improve the performance and functionality of our Site.
  • Functionality Cookies: These cookies allow our Site to remember your preferences (such as language or region) and provide enhanced, more personalized features. For example, they may be used to remember login details or other settings you have chosen.
  • Targeting/Advertising Cookies: These cookies are used to deliver advertisements that are relevant to you. They also help measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns. We may use third-party services like Google Analytics and social media platforms to collect these cookies for targeted advertising purposes.
  • Analytics Cookies: These cookies collect information about your activity on the Site (e.g., which pages you visit and how long you stay). This information helps us analyze Site usage, track user behavior, and improve our services. We may use third-party analytics tools such as Google Analytics to assist us in this.

3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to achieve the following purposes:

  • Enhance User Experience: Cookies help us personalize your experience on the Site by remembering your preferences, such as language selection or user settings.
  • Improve Site Performance: We use performance cookies to analyze how our Site is used, identify issues, and optimize the user experience.
  • Provide Targeted Advertising: We may use cookies to show ads that are relevant to you and to track the effectiveness of advertising campaigns.
  • Gather Analytics: Analytics cookies allow us to gather information on how users engage with our Site, which enables us to make improvements and understand how to best serve our visitors.

4. Third-Party Cookies

In addition to our own cookies, we may also use third-party cookies provided by trusted partners. These third-party services may use cookies to help us track user activity and provide personalized content, ads, or analytics.

Some third-party services we may use include:

  • Google Analytics: For tracking and analyzing Site traffic.
  • Social Media Platforms: Such as Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, etc., to help provide social sharing features and tailor advertising content.

Please note that we do not control these third-party cookies, and they are governed by the privacy policies of the third-party service providers.

5. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have the right to manage and control the cookies placed on your device. Below are options to manage your cookie preferences:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can set your browser to block cookies or notify you when cookies are being used. However, please note that blocking cookies may impact your ability to use certain features of the Site.
  • Opt-Out of Targeted Advertising: You can opt-out of targeted advertising by visiting the following websites:
    • YourAdChoices
    • Network Advertising Initiative (NAI)
    • Google Ad Settings
  • Disabling Analytics Cookies: You can opt-out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Please note that opting out of some cookies may affect your user experience on the Site or limit certain functionalities.

6. Cookie Consent

When you visit our Site for the first time, you will be presented with a cookie banner that informs you of our use of cookies and asks for your consent. You can give your consent by clicking "Accept" or continue browsing our Site. If you wish to reject cookies, you can select the "Decline" option, and cookies will not be placed on your device, unless they are essential for the operation of the Site.

7. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any changes will be reflected by an updated "Effective Date" at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this Cookie Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are using cookies.
