Our leadership team

Sarah Johnson

Chief Editor, PhD in English Literature, Senior Manuscript Consultant

Sarah, a PhD graduate in English Literature, is a seasoned manuscript editor, mentor, and consultant specializing in offering valuable insights to authors and publishing teams.

With a strong foundation in Literary Theory and Linguistics, coupled with an MFA in Creative Writing, she garnered extensive experience in academic publishing and editorial development over 12 years. Her notable roles include serving as senior editor at international publishing houses Penguin Random House and Oxford University Press.

Michael Chen

Technical Editor, MS in Computer Science, Technical Documentation Specialist

Michael, an MS graduate in Computer Science, is a meticulous technical editor with expertise in enhancing complex technical and scientific manuscripts for clarity and precision.

With dual backgrounds in Engineering and Technical Communication, he has spent 8 years refining documentation for major tech companies and academic publishers. His skillset includes specialized editing for computer science journals, technical manuals, and scientific publications requiring technical accuracy.

Elena Rodriguez

Managing Editor, MBA, Publishing Operations Director

Elena, an MBA graduate with a background in Publishing Management, leads our editorial operations with a focus on efficiency, quality control, and client satisfaction.

Combining her expertise in business operations with 10 years in the publishing industry, Elena has developed streamlined workflows that ensure timely delivery without compromising editorial quality. She previously managed editorial departments at major academic journals and has implemented innovative quality assurance protocols for manuscript processing.
