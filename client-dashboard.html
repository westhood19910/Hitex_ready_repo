Our Service Levels

At HiTex EdiTex, we understand that editing needs vary from one manuscript to another. Thatâ€™s why we offer three distinct service levels, each designed to support the specific goals of each client. Each level provides a targeted approach, ranging from surface-level corrections to in-depth language restructuring to help you choose the precise degree of editorial input based on the nature and stage of your work.

Standard Editing Level

Ideal for authors seeking language polishing and consistency across subject matter. This foundational level ensures your document reads clearly and professionally, without altering the original meaning or structure. It focuses on correcting:

  • Grammar, punctuation, and syntax errors
  • Spelling and sentence-level inconsistencies
  • Coherence and logical flow between ideas
  • Accurate use of discipline-specific terminology

Recommended for: Early drafts, internal reports, general academic manuscripts, conference papers.

Get Started

Advanced Editing Level

For authors who need both language correction and deeper structural refinement. In addition to everything offered under Standard Editing, this level involves substantive editorial work to improve the overall organization and presentation of your document. Advanced Editing includes:

  • In-depth editing for structure, clarity, and narrative flow
  • Improved transitions, paragraph organization, and academic tone
  • Adjustments for formality and field-specific expectations
  • Formatting and styling based on institutional, journal, or publisher guidelines.

Recommended for: Research manuscripts for journal submission, thesis and dissertation drafts, policy briefs, technical documentation.

Get Started

Premium Editing Level

Our most comprehensive editing service, offering expert-level content refinement and end-to-end manuscript readiness. Premium Editing is designed for authors preparing a final version for publication, peer review, or high-impact audiences. This level combines all features of Standard and Advanced Editing with:

  • Peer review by a subject-matter expert to evaluate content relevance, scientific merit, and alignment with your target audience or journal
  • A detailed Editorial Report outlining revisions made, editorial rationale, and feedback on publication readiness
  • Free multiple-round editing support for any revisions made during the lifecycle of the project (offered within the project timeline, up to the agreed submission or publishing milestone)

Recommended for: Final research submissions, grant proposals, journal-ready manuscripts, white papers, and policy documents.
