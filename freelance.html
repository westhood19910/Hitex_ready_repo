Qualifications & Requirements

Since 2004, we've been working with leading academic publishers to provide exceptional editorial services. With decades of experience in manuscript editing, we offer a range of requirements to ensure the highest quality results for your academic publication.

Education

Do you have the right academic background? We require a PhD or Master's degree in a relevant field. Candidates with degrees in English, Communications, or subject-specific expertise are strongly preferred for our editorial team.

Experience

A stunning editorial portfolio alone is not enough. We focus on a minimum of 3 years of manuscript editing experience in academic settings with scholarly publications. Demonstrated familiarity with academic writing styles and conventions is essential.

Language Proficiency

We design and develop content that is user-friendly and visually appealing. Native English speakers or equivalent proficiency are required, with excellent command of grammar, syntax, and academic writing conventions for conversions.

Technical Skills

We build your editorial process with conversion in mind. Proficiency in Microsoft Word track changes, LaTeX, and other editing software is required. Familiarity with academic citation styles (APA, MLA, Chicago, etc.) and document formatting tools is essential.
