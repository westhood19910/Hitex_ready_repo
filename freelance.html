Why Join Our Freelance Community?

Global Impact

Your expertise helps researchers and EFL and ESL authors communicate their discoveries and ideas to the world. Every project you complete contributes to bridging the gap between research and real-world impact.

Diverse Disciplines

We work with editors from varied scientific and technical backgrounds—including medicine, engineering, mathematics and computing, business and finance, and life and social sciences. Your specialization and knowledge has a place here.

Human + AI Synergy

We combine human expertise with advanced AI tools to deliver precise, insightful, and impactful communication. Our freelancers gain access to these cutting-edge tools, making editing more efficient and rewarding.

Flexibility & Growth

Whether you're looking for steady projects or occasional assignments, our freelance program adapts to your schedule. We also offer continuous learning opportunities to refine your editing skills and expand your professional portfolio.

What We Look For

We seek highly skilled freelancers who are:

  • Experienced in scientific, technical, or academic editing
  • Proficient in grammar, clarity, and adherence to journal guidelines
  • Familiar with scholarly communication standards (APA, AMA, Vancouver, etc.)
  • Passionate about helping researchers succeed
  • Reliable, detail-oriented, and capable of meeting deadlines

If you have a background in research, publishing, or scientific writing, you'll feel right at home in our team. We value expertise, dedication, and a commitment to excellence in scholarly communication.
