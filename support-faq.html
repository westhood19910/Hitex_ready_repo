Services & Ordering

Our standard turnaround time is 3-5 business days. We also offer express options for faster delivery (24-hour and 48-hour) at an additional cost, which you can select when placing your order.

We offer several levels of editing. **Proofreading** is best for final drafts needing a check for typos and grammatical errors. **Substantive Editing** is ideal for manuscripts that require improvements in clarity, flow, and structure. Please visit our Services page for a detailed comparison.

Payment & Billing

The Editing Process

You'll receive an email notification once your document is ready. It will be available for download from your secure client portal. We will provide two versions: one with "Track Changes" to show all edits and a "clean" version with all changes accepted.

Technical Support

We primarily work with Microsoft Word files (.doc, .docx). This allows us to use the "Track Changes" feature. If your manuscript is in another format like LaTeX or PDF, please contact our support team before ordering to discuss options.
