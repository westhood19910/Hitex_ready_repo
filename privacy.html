Privacy Policy

HiTex EdiTex ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. 1. Information We Collect

We collect both personal and non-personal data through your interaction with our Site and services. The types of information we collect include, but are not limited to:

Personal Information

Personal information is data that can be used to identify you directly or indirectly. We may collect the following personal information when you use our Site or engage with our services:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, shipping/billing address
  • Payment Information: Credit card details, payment methods, billing address
  • Transaction Information: Order history, purchase details, and other transactional data
  • Communications: Any information you provide to us when you contact us, submit inquiries, or participate in surveys or promotions
  • Account Information: If applicable, user account login credentials, usernames, and passwords

Non-Personal Information

Non-personal information refers to data that does not identify you personally but is collected automatically as you interact with our Site. This may include:

  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, and network information
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, links clicked, referring URLs, and time spent on the Site
  • Cookies and Tracking Data: Information collected through cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies used to personalize and enhance your experience

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the personal and non-personal information we collect for the following legitimate purposes:

  • To Provide and Improve Our Services: Processing orders, managing transactions, providing customer support, personalizing your experience, and improving the functionality of our Site.
  • To Communicate with You: Responding to inquiries, sending updates about your orders, and sending marketing communications (where you have opted in).
  • For Marketing and Promotional Purposes: With your consent, we may send newsletters, offers, and promotional materials about our services. You can opt-out of these communications at any time.
  • To Comply with Legal Obligations: We may use your information to comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes, and to protect our rights and the rights of others.
  • For Fraud Prevention: We use your information to detect and prevent fraudulent transactions or other illegal activities.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to track the usage of our Site, enhance your browsing experience, and provide personalized content. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings, but note that disabling cookies may limit your ability to use certain features of our Site.

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Session Cookies: These cookies are temporary and are deleted once you close your browser.
  • Persistent Cookies: These cookies remain on your device for a set period of time to remember your preferences for future visits.
  • Third-Party Cookies: These cookies are placed by third-party services (such as Google Analytics or social media platforms) for tracking and advertising purposes.

For more details, please refer to our Cookie Policy.
